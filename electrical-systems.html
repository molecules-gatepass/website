Substation Expertise

We specialize in designing and constructing robust electrical substations that form the backbone of steel plant power systems. Our expertise covers the entire spectrum of substation development:

  • High-Voltage Substations: Up to 400kV capacity for main plant power supply
  • Distribution Substations: Medium voltage distribution throughout the facility
  • GIS Technology: Gas-insulated switchgear for compact, reliable operations
  • Transformer Systems: Power transformers with advanced monitoring
  • Protection Systems: Comprehensive relay protection and automation
  • SCADA Integration: Remote monitoring and control capabilities
